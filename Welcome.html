Vakaypic(k)s is intended to be used as a trip planner. Currently, users are able to enter a date and location and then view photos (courtesy of Instagram) that fit the two criteria. The intention is to help undecided vacationers make a decision about their next vacation.

Instructions:

    Enter the location(s) that you are considering
    Enter a period from which you would like to view photos of your desired location
    Enjoy browsing the photos that will hopefully help you decide whether this location is the ideal vacation spot for you!
